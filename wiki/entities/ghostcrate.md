# ghostcrate

A self-hosted registry for [[Rust]] crates, built for private teams, homelabs,
and internal platform environments that want crates on infrastructure they
control while supporting Cargo-compatible publishing and installs. By
[[GhostKellz]].

The Cargo-registry counterpart to [[ghostport]] (OCI/Docker registry) in the
[[Ghost Ecosystem]] self-hosted-registry theme.

Built on [[Rust]].
