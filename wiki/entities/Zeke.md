# Zeke

Local-first AI routing, caching, usage tracking, and provider transport for
[[Rust]] agents. By [[GhostKellz]].

Zeke operates at the AI-provider transport/routing layer, making it a natural
companion to [[Jarvis]] (which handles orchestration and memory). Where Jarvis
drives CLI tools and remembers context, Zeke focuses on the routing/caching/
transport plumbing for Rust-based agents.

Built on [[Rust]].
