# GhostView

A sleek, modular desktop GUI for discovering and managing packages across the
Arch Linux ecosystem, built with [[Rust]] and `egui`. Powered by [[ghostbrew]]
under the hood. By [[GhostKellz]].

Browses and manages Pacman (system/official), AUR and Chaotic-AUR, Flatpak
(Flathub + custom remotes), and KDE/GNOME apps. The GUI counterpart to the
[[ghostbrew]] backend in the [[Ghost Ecosystem]] Arch tooling.

Built on [[Rust]].
