# GhostChain

A next-generation Web5 blockchain platform with integrated identity services,
built as a [[Rust]] microservices monorepo workspace. By [[GhostKellz]]; part of
the [[Ghost Ecosystem]].

## Highlights

- GQUIC transport (the QUIC stack, related to [[zquic]]).
- Post-quantum cryptography (sharing primitives with [[zcrypto]]).
- GhostPlane L2 integration.
- Workspace layout: `core/` (ghostchain-core) and per-service crates.

Built on [[Rust]].
