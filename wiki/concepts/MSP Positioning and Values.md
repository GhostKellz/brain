# MSP Positioning and Values

## Definition

This is the public brand positioning of [[CK Technology Solutions]] as expressed on
[[cktechx.com]]: an MSP that brings secure, modern, enterprise-grade IT to small and
mid-sized businesses, paired with personal, founder-led service that large providers
"can't match." It is the why-choose-us story sitting beneath the four service lines.

## Mission

"Deliver IT that works." Technology should enable a business, not frustrate it — so
the company offers reliable, secure, modern solutions with personal service, partnering
with growing businesses to handle everything from day-to-day support to strategic
infrastructure planning.

## The Three Core Values

- **Security First** — security built in as a foundation, not an afterthought (see
  [[Network and Security Services]]).
- **Personal Service** — direct access to engineers who know the client's environment;
  "no ticket queues, no runaround."
- **Modern Solutions** — cloud-first thinking, automation, and proactive management.

## Founder-Led, Team-Backed

The site is explicit that the company is founder-led: [[GhostKellz]] (Christopher
Kelley), a systems and infrastructure engineer with a Computer Science degree,
founded it to bring enterprise-grade technology to New England SMBs "without the
bloat, the runaround, or the faceless support queues." The About page links to his
personal site, [[ckelley.dev]].

## Region & Approach

- **Region:** based in New Hampshire, serving New England — NH, MA, RI, ME — with the
  pitch of faster response and local on-site help.
- **Approach:** no one-size-fits-all; understand the business first, then build "the
  right solution — not the most expensive one," acting as a trusted extension of the
  client's team.

> [!key-insight]
> The differentiator is *proximity and accountability* — a named founder and direct
> engineer access — set against the impersonal feel of large national providers. The
> three values map directly onto the service lines (Security First →
> [[Network and Security Services]]; Personal Service → [[Managed IT Services]]).

> [!gap]
> This is marketing-level positioning only. No client names, case studies, internal
> tooling, pricing specifics, or team details beyond the public founder story are
> recorded — the vault is public.
