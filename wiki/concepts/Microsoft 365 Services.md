# Microsoft 365 Services

## Definition

Microsoft 365 is one of the four core services [[CK Technology LLC]] markets on
[[cktechx.com]]: deployment, security hardening, and ongoing management of a client's
M365 tenant. The framing is "more than just email" — helping businesses use the full
platform while keeping strong security controls.

## What the Service Includes (public marketing)

- **Deployment & migration** — clean migrations from other platforms or on-prem
  Exchange.
- **Security hardening** — MFA, conditional access, and data-protection policies.
- **Teams & collaboration** — Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive setup/optimization.
- **License optimization** — right-sizing licenses to avoid overpaying.
- **Ongoing management** — user provisioning, troubleshooting, tenant administration.
- **Training & adoption** — helping teams actually use the tools.

## Positioning

The page stresses a balance of **security and usability** — "security that doesn't
slow you down" — implementing M365 controls that protect data without frustrating
users.

> [!key-insight]
> Security hardening (MFA, conditional access) sits inside the productivity offering,
> echoing the company-wide "Security First" value in [[MSP Positioning and Values]].
> License optimization is the cost-discipline angle that recurs across CK Technology's
> services.
