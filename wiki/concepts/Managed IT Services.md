# Managed IT Services

## Definition

Managed IT is the core offering of [[CK Technology Solutions]], marketed on
[[cktechx.com]]: proactive monitoring, maintenance, and support delivered for a
predictable monthly cost. The pitch is to stop *chasing* IT problems and start
*preventing* them, with the MSP acting as an extension of the client's team.

## What the Service Includes (public marketing)

- **24/7 monitoring** of systems, networks, and endpoints to catch issues early.
- **Helpdesk support** with fast response from real technicians.
- **Patch management** — automated updates and security patches.
- **Endpoint management** for workstations, laptops, and mobile devices.
- **Backup monitoring** with daily verification and test restores.
- **Vendor coordination** with the client's software/hardware vendors.

## Commercial Model

Plans are priced **per-user or per-device, everything included** — positioned as "no
hidden fees, no surprise bills." This flat, predictable pricing is a recurring theme
across the site's services.

> [!key-insight]
> The framing is preventive, not reactive: monitoring + patching + backup
> verification are sold as the things that stop incidents before they reach the
> helpdesk. Predictable per-seat pricing reinforces the "trusted partner, not a
> meter" positioning in [[MSP Positioning and Values]].
