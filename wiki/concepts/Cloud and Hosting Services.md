# Cloud and Hosting Services

## Definition

Cloud & Hosting is one of the four core services [[CK Technology Solutions]] markets on
[[cktechx.com]]: helping businesses move to, optimize, and manage cloud infrastructure
and managed hosting, with a stated focus on reliability, security, and
cost-effectiveness rather than chasing trends.

## What the Service Includes (public marketing)

- **Cloud migration** — planning and execution to move workloads with minimal
  disruption.
- **Managed hosting** — fully managed virtual servers and infrastructure with
  monitoring, patching, and support.
- **Backup & recovery** — cloud-based backup with verified restores and continuity
  planning.
- **Hybrid solutions** — mixed on-premises + cloud designs.
- **Infrastructure management** — ongoing optimization and cost control.
- **Virtual Desktop (VDI)** — secure remote desktops for distributed teams.

## Positioning

The page emphasizes a "modern infrastructure, practical approach" — public cloud,
private hosting, or hybrid chosen to fit actual business needs, built on
"enterprise-grade infrastructure from trusted providers" combined with the MSP's own
managed hosting capabilities.
